FAQ
How does the commission process work? ▾
You fill out the brief above, selecting your preset, engine, style, and scope. I reply within 48 hours with a precise quote. Once we align on scope and price, I send a contract and invoice. Work begins after deposit.
What deliverables do I receive? ▾
You receive a fully configured project file for your engine (UE5 or Unity), including all terrain assets, materials, and technical setup. RAW delivery includes source exports (heightmaps, splat maps, textures) ready for your own integration.
Do you offer revisions? ▾
Yes. Terrain S and M include 2 revision rounds, Terrain L includes 3, and Terrain XL includes unlimited revisions. Revisions are scoped to changes within the agreed brief — major scope changes are quoted separately.
Can I use the terrain commercially? ▾
By default, commissions include a single-project commercial license. Exclusivity (full IP transfer) is available as an add-on and is reflected in the pricing estimator above.
What if my project doesn't fit a preset? ▾
Use the Terrain XL preset or select 'Custom' in the quantity selector to describe your scope. I'll review your brief and come back with a tailored quote after a short scoping call.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
Bank transfer (IBAN) and PayPal. A 30% deposit is required to begin, with the remainder invoiced on delivery. For XL-scope projects, a milestone-based payment schedule is agreed during scoping.
