About me

I'm a self-taught environment artist who's spent over a decade obsessing over terrain, atmosphere, and the kind of digital landscapes that make you stop and look twice. It started as a personal curiosity: tweaking game settings, modding levels, breaking things just to understand how they worked.

That curiosity grew into a craft, and the craft grew into Kalysteon, a catalogue of production-ready environment packs built for developers who care about quality. Six years of selling assets on Fab, Unity Asset Store and ArtStation. Every pack is the result of accumulated time, workflows refined through obsession, and details no one asked for but everyone notices.

I focus on world building, the art of making a space feel real, inhabited, and worth exploring. Not just pretty renders, but environments that hold up in motion, under dynamic lighting, at any scale.

"Not a studio. Just someone who cares deeply about the craft."
