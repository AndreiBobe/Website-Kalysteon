Etymology
KALYSTEON
Kalos+Eon

Kalos (καλός): Greek for beautiful. Also the root of calisthenics, kalos and sthenos, beauty and strength, the art of mastering form through the body alone.

Eon: an era, a geological age. The span of time that actually shapes a place.

That's how Kalysteon came to me. The T was already there in calisthenics, sitting between kalos and sthenos. I kept it as a natural bridge into eon. Nothing forced, it was in the root all along.
